What I Learned from Digital Literacy

I learned the critical importance of managing my digital footprint responsibly to protect my privacy, security, and online reputation. Specific strategies I gained knowledge of include:

  • Regularly checking the privacy and security settings on all my social media profiles and apps
  • Being diligent about clearing my cookies, browsing history, and cached files across all my devices
  • Cautiously avoiding oversharing personal contact details, addresses, schedules, or other sensitive information publicly online
  • Using strong unique passwords for each account with a password manager along with multi-factor authentication when available

I also learned several effective search techniques that will allow me to improve my online research and ability to locate accurate, credible information efficiently:

  • Identifying key concepts or topics and determining important keywords and search terms
  • Leveraging advanced Google operators to narrow down search results
  • Evaluating factors like source expertise, recency, evidence provided, and agreement from multiple reputable sites to ascertain credibility

For improving my email communication, I learned several best practices:

  • Crafting concise, descriptive subject lines and starting with a proper greeting
  • Being concise yet thorough while tailoring the information to what the recipient needs to know
  • Signing off with an appropriate closing and my name

I gained an introduction to the field of artificial intelligence and some awareness of key machine learning concepts. I learned about the distinction between weak/narrow AI and strong AI, supervised and unsupervised learning, and the risks and ethical considerations surrounding AI systems.

What I have already used:

  • Started using more secure password habits and enabling multi-factor authentication
  • Become more diligent about regularly checking and clearing cookies, browsing history, and cache
  • Avoided publicly sharing personal contact details on social media or other public-facing sites
  • Applied email best practices for clearer communication

What I plan to use in the future:

  • Continue monitoring and refining privacy settings on social media profiles and apps
  • Systematically apply search strategy knowledge for better online research
  • Spread digital literacy awareness and provide feedback on AI development
  • Guide others' digital literacy where appropriate in my community

In summary, I learned many practical skills and mindsets around online safety, effective searching, clear communication, and responsible use of emerging technology. With ongoing refinement and sharing of knowledge, I can help shape an ethical digital future.
